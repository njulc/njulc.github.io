About

Currently I am a first-year PhD student of School of Artificial Intelligence in Nanjing University advised by Prof. Chao Qian and a member of LAMDA Group, led by Prof. Zhi-Hua Zhou.

My current research interest lies in self-evolving LLM agents and automated algorithm design with their applications in industrial scenarios (e.g., electronic design automation).
I am also committed to researching foundational methodologies in the field of artificial intelligence, such as evolutionary learning and reinforcement learning.
